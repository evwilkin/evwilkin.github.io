About Evan

I love creating new things. I started my path to Web Development after working at several different tech companies making the leap from startup-mode to large companies. When I saw the consistent problems customers encountered with each company's online presence, I thought I can do this better. Since diving in head first, I've grown addicted to the constant learning required in this field and the new challenges you find yourself tackling every day. My goal on any project is to make an experience that uniquely represents a brand to their customers and delivers a visually appealing, interactive online presence.
