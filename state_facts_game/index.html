Most laws we know are easy to understand, make sense, and are even obvious to us.

Some, however, are lesser known and leave you wondering "who came up with this?"

Grab a friend and get ready to guess which actual laws apply to which state!

Believe it or not, all of these are real laws - but only one law applies to each state. Guess correctly and get a point - take turns, first to 5 right answers wins!
